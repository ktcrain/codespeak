This tool flags language likely to be harmful, hateful, stigmatizing, or risky under TikTok-style moderation policies, then suggests clearer and more respectful alternatives. It does not provide an exhaustive list of platform-banned words — platforms do not publish such lists and enforcement depends on context. Sources: TikTok Community Guidelines, GLAAD Media Reference Guide, NCDJ Disability Language Style Guide, APA Inclusive Language Guide.

⚙ Deep Scan — NLP Settings

Deep Scan sends your text to an external AI API to detect issues that regex can't catch (context-dependent slurs, implicit bias, novel slang). Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it is never sent to our servers.
